About My Favorite Video Games


Marvel Rivals

One of my favorite games currently is Marvel Rivals. I love highly competitive games where I feel awarded for outplaying my enemies by either mechanical skill or by simply knowing the game better then my opponent. I also just like using the characters I see in Marvel movies and can imagine myself doing those exact things in this game.


Valorant

Another favorite of mine is Valorant. The tactical gameplay and team coordination was something that encapsulated me when I first got into the game. I played the game so much that I eventually got into the top 0.17% of players in the game!
